The Defence Media Operations Centre (DMOC)

Defence Media Operations Centre supports MOD media and communications at all levels.

The Defence Media Operations Centre (DMOC) supports the media and communications (M&C) effect at all levels, through the delivery of world class training as well as a highly trained rapidly deployable M&C capability.

At the centre of this capability is the rapidly deployable Joint Media Operations Team (JMOT) and the Defence School of Media & Communications (DSMC) responsible for the overall co-ordination, development and delivery of M&C training and education. The DMOC also provides M&C consultancy across the defence community.

With a balance of 41 Staff from the Royal Navy (RN), Army, Royal Air Force (RAF) and the Civil Service, the DMOC reports to the Director Media and Communications (DMC) at the Ministry of Defence (MOD) and is in all respects a Tri-service asset.

DMOC Logistics Support Wing

Logistics support to worldwide media operations, both emerging and enduring operations, exercise and routine support to the DMOC.

The DMOC’s logistics support wing, like the rest of DMOC, is staffed by representatives from all the three Services of the Armed Forces.

The logistic support wing supplies media operations support to individuals and units on operational duties, the Joint Media Operations Team (JMOT) and the Defence School of Media & Communications (DSMC) with specialist IT and communication systems, stills and video equipment, technical training, vehicles and life support equipment for deployment anywhere in the world.

The Joint Media Operations Team (JMOT)

The JMOT is Defence’s only standing, very high readiness, and rapidly deployable Media Ops capability.

Consisting of personnel from all three services, the team provides expert support to commanders at all levels. It is highly trained in all aspects of Media & Communications activities and is equipped to be self-sufficient in the field.

The JMOT is designed to be scalable, depending on the requirement. It has the flexibility to be able to deploy at any level from individual augmentees to a fully equipped Media Operations Centre (MOC), providing the full range of media planning, analysis, briefing, escorting and spokesman roles.

Members of the team deploy regularly to Afghanistan, augmenting the in-theatre Media Ops organisations as required. The team also maintains the ability to deploy at 48-hours notice in support of contingency operations as part of the UK’s Joint Rapid Reaction Force, in Military Aid to Civil Authority tasks and in response to directives from the MOD. They also provide support to other goverment departments such as the FCO and DfiD.

Defence School of Media & Communications (DSMC)

The DSMC is a centre of excellence for all M&C training, education and consultancy. It is aligned to FCO and DFiD training and offers scheduled and bespoke training courses for personnel in Defence who work in M&C related posts, or are required to engage with the media.

Individual Training: DSMC offers a range of individual training packages and courses to equip individuals with the skill-sets to perform a variety of M&C related roles. Training includes:

  • Media Specialist Training
  • Media Skills Training
  • Combat Camera Team Training

Support to Operations: DSMC supports the Front Line Commands in the delivery of pre-deployment and collective training to those deploying on operations.

Through Life Training Support: DSMC provides Through Life Training Support to the Defence Academy at Shrivenham delivering M&C related training to a variety of command and staff courses.

International Training: DSMC can deliver training and M&C support to NATO, EU and other international partners. The school has recently delivered training in Afghanistan, Uganda and Germany among others. Requests for training from outside of the MOD must be staffed through the International Defence Training (IDT) system, or via DMC.
