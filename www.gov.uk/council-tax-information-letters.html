Introduction

This guide contains a series of letters that provide information about Council Tax.

2012

Council Tax Information Letter 2/2012: potential Council Tax discount on empty homes

This Council Tax information letter covers the topic of Council Tax discount on empty homes. It provides clarification on the proposed discounts for properties that are unoccupied and unfurnished.

Council Tax (Discount Disregards) Amendment Order 2011: Service personnel and second homes

This Council Tax information letter covers the topic of Service personnel and second homes. As part of its commitment to the Military Covenant, the Government is keen to make authorities aware of the position regarding Service personnel to ensure they are treated fairly and not disadvantaged when applying for a Council Tax second homes discount.
