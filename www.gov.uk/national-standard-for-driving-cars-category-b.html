National standard for driving cars (category B)

The standard sets out what the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) believes is needed to be a safe and responsible car driver.

Only applies to England, Scotland and Wales

Changes to the standard

DSA updates the standard on a regular basis. If any safety critical issues are identified, it’s updated straight away. You can keep up to date with new versions by email.

Sign up for DSA email alerts

Further information

You can contact DSA if you have:

  • questions about the standard, syllabus or competence framework
  • ideas about how they could be improved
  • evidence, from research or practice, that will make the standards more robust
