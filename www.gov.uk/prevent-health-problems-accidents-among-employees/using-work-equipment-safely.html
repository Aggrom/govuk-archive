Part 5: Using work equipment safely

Work equipment covers any equipment used while at work, including:

  • machines - eg circular saws, photocopiers, tractors
  • hand tools - eg screwdrivers, knives, meat cleavers
  • lifting equipment - eg vehicle hoists, lifting slings, bath lifts
  • other equipment - eg ladders, water pressure cleaners

All new equipment must meet European safety standards

For more information, download the Health and Safety Executive’s brochure on the safe use of work equipment.

Download ‘Using work equipment safely’ (PDF, 194KB)

Assessing equipment

Examine the equipment you or your employees use, look at the risks and think of ways to reduce or remove those risks.

Work equipment must be:

  • suitable for use
  • regularly maintained
  • inspected (before first use and then regularly)
  • assessed for noise and vibration

Training employees

Prevent staff from endangering themselves or others by making sure:

  • they’re properly trained to use the equipment
  • instructions are in plain English

Buying new equipment

Make sure any new equipment you buy meets safety standards.
