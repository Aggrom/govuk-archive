Set up and run a motorcycle approved training body (ATB)

Introduction

The ATB manual gives detailed information for ATBs and instructors about what they must do to meet the legal requirements for providing:

  • compulsory basic training (CBT)
  • direct access scheme training

The document must be made available to all instructors within every ATB. It should be read in conjunction with the conditions of appointment under which ATBs are authorised.
