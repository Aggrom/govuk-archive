Transfer of 1981 and 1982 FCO files to The National Archives

The Foreign and Commonwealth Office is required under the Public Records Act to transfer files selected for permanent preservation to The National Archives (TNA) at the 30-year mark. Under the provisions of the Constitutional Reform and Governance Act 2010 (section 45) this will reduce progressively to 20 years by 2022.

The FCO is currently in the process of transferring around 18,000 colonial administration files to the TNA. Over the 2011-2012 period, we have had to redeploy many of the resources normally committed to the 30-year transfer of files onto the colonial administration files project. This has resulted in a backlog of 1981 and 1982 files awaiting review and preparation for transfer.

In 2012 we have prioritised the transfer of material relating to the Falklands War. The following FCO files will be available at the TNA’s New Year opening in 2013:
