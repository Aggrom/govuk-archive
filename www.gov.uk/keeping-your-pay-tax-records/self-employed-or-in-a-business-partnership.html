Part 4: Self employed or in a business partnership

Keep your business and personal finances separate so you can accurately complete each part of your tax return.

Business records

You must keep records that show all your:

  • sales and takings
  • business purchases and expenses

This might include:

  • business assets youâ€™ve bought (stock, equipment)
  • value of stock and work in progress at the end of your accounting period
  • details of payments to employees (wages, expenses, benefits)
  • business vehicle and travel costs
  • interest from any bank or building society accounts
  • other money coming in, eg money you invest in your business
