Part 2: How to apply

You need:

  • one application for each premises - to register more than 5 premises at a time (known as “bulk applications”) apply by post
  • the Standard Industrial Classification Code (SICC) used to classify your business type
  • your company number - if your business is a limited company

Register online with the Environment Agency.

Other ways to apply

You can also apply by post or telephone.

Single premises form

Download ‘Form HWRP01 - hazardous waste registration single premises’ (PDF, 350KB) Download ‘Guidance - HWRP01’ (PDF, 70KB)

Bulk premises forms

Use form HWRB01 and the ‘List of premises’ template. This list will need to be sent in a format called XML.

Download ‘Form HWRB01 - hazardous waste registration multiple premises’ (PDF, 300KB) Download ‘Guidance - HWRB01’ (PDF, 70KB) Download ‘Template for listing your premises’ (XLS, 650KB) Download ‘Guidance - how to list your premises’ (PDF, 200KB)
