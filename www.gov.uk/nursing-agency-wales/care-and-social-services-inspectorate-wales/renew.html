Licence Nursing agency registration (Wales)

It is not currently possible to apply online for this licence. Please contact your local authority.

Overview

To run a nursing agency in Wales you must register it, meet certain standards and operate according to the current regulations.

The minimum standards and the regulations make sure the agency is managed properly and provides quality care.

Download ‘Nursing agencies: minimum standards’ document (PDF, 105KB)

For information on the other legal conditions you must meet, such as quality of service and premises and record-keeping, see ‘Regulations for nurses’ agencies’ on the Care and Social Services Inspectorate Wales (CSSIW) website.

You must also read The Nurses Agencies (Wales) Regulations 2003.

Restrictions

If you want to operate or manage more than one agency you must make a separate application for each one.

How to register

You must contact your local CSSIW office to register. Find your local office on their webiste.

You must pay an annual fee of £500.

Fines and penalties

If you don’t register, don’t meet the minimum standards or break the regulations, you could be taken to court.
