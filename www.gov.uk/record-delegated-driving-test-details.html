Service Record delegated driving test details

Not what you're looking for? ↓

Record the details of scheduled delegated driving tests. You must be a registered delegated driving examiner or administrator authorised by the delegated examiner.

What you need to know

Start now on the Department for Transport website

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know

Tests you must record

You must record the details of the following tests:

  • licence acquisition tests
  • Driver Certificate of Professional Competence (CPC) practical tests

Download ‘A guide to delegated examiner test notifications’ (PDF, 745KB)

Paying for Driver CPC tests

You have to pay to record Driver CPC test details. You can pay in 2 ways:

  • by debit or credit card
  • by debit from a pre-funded account

A pre-funded account lets you deposit funds with the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) in advance and pay the fee by debiting the amount directly from your account.

Apply for a pre-funded account

To apply for a pre-funded account, download the terms and conditions, sign the document and send it to the DSA.

Download ‘Use of pre-funded accounts for DSA services - terms and conditions’ (PDF, 35KB)
