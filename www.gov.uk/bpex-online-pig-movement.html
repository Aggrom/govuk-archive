Service Electronic licence to report pig movements

Not what you're looking for? ↓

Apply online with the British Pig Executive (BPEX) for an electronic licence for pig movements (eAML2).

What you need to know

Start now on the eaML2 website

  • Before you start
  • Other ways to apply

The eAML2 combines the original paper form AML2 and the Food Chain Information forms.

Copies of the AML2 are automatically uploaded to Trading Standards so you no longer need to post them.

You will need:

  • your membership number if you’re in an assurance scheme
  • the herdmark, holding number and site postcode if you’re not in an assurance scheme

If you’re in Scotland you should register with the ScotEID national pig movement database instead.

Cross compliance

These standards are part of cross compliance. You must meet them to get your farming subsidy under the Single Payment Scheme.

You can report pig movements by phone and get advice by email.

eAML2 helpline
eaml2@bpex.org.uk
Telephone: 0844 335 8400
Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm
Calls cost 5p per minute from landlines

Some organisations act as agent’s and can report pig movements for you. Contact the eAML2 helpline for more information.
