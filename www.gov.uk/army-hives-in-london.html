Army HIVES in London

Find and contact army HIVES (forces information centres) in London.

A list of army HIVES in London, with contact details (including phone numbers) and opening times. HIVES are information centres for forces personnel and their families.

Central London

Serving Earl’s Court, Hyde Park, Notting Hill, Putney, Wandsworth, Victoria, Wellington Barracks, Regent’s Park, St John’s Wood, Whetstone, Wimbledon, Headley Court and Uxbridge.

London Central HIVE is not currently manned. Emails will be monitored by Windsor HIVE. Please email enquiries to windsorhiveinfo@armymail.mod.uk

The Central London HIVE covers all central London-based military units and Service Family Accommodation (SFA) areas. This HIVE also provides relocation information and signposting for personnel and their dependants living in Whetstone, Uxbridge, Headley Court and numerous small SFA and unit locations in London.

Central London HIVE
Block C Regents Park Barracks
Albany Street
London NW1 4AL

East and South London: Woolwich HIVE

Serving Woolwich, Biggin Hill, Fort Halstead, Croydon and Coulsdon.

The Woolwich and East London HIVE is located near Woolwich Barracks and covers the eastern side of London stretching south just into Kent and the Croydon SFA area.

Woolwich is a garrison town and is home to the Royal Artillery Museum. The barracks and SFA are located a short distance from the Greenwich Market, the Millennium Dome, the 2012 Olympic park and the Cutty Sark.

Woolwich HIVE
Woolwich Community Centre
Royal Artillery Barracks
Woollwich
London
SE18 4BB

  • Email: woolwichhiveinfo@armymail.mod.uk
  • Telephone: 0208 7813622

West London: Windsor HIVE

Serving Windsor, Chertsey, West Byfleet, Hounslow, Kingston, Shepperton and Twickenham.

The Windsor and West London areas covered by this HIVE stretch from Combermere and Victoria Barracks in Berkshire, across to the west London Borough of Kingston and south to Surrey and the SFA in West Byfleet.

The main Units, barracks and SFA locations covered are Windsor, Cavalry Barracks Hounslow, Shepperton, Chertsey, the Royal Military School of Music at Kneller Hall in Twickenham and Feltham.

66 Furness Way

  • Telephone: 01753 832285
  • Fax: 01753 832514
