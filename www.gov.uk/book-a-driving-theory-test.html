Service Book your driving theory test

Book your official driving theory test - you’ll need your provisional driving licence.

What you need to know

Start now on the Directgov website

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know
  • Other ways to apply

You’ll need your:

  • Great Britain or Northern Ireland provisional driving licence number
  • email address to get your booking confirmation - you can’t book online if you don’t have one

You have to wait at least 3 clear working days before taking your test again if you don’t pass.

  • Takes around 10 minutes
  • Debit or credit card required

Book by phone

You can book your theory test by calling the Driving Standards Agency (DSA). If you don’t have an email address, your confirmation will be sent in the post.

DSA theory test bookings

Telephone: 0300 200 1122 (English), 0300 200 1133 (Welsh)
Textphone: 0300 200 1166
Monday to Friday, 8am to 4pm

Book by post

To book your theory test by post download the application form and send it with your payment to the DSA.

You can pay by cheque or postal order - make your cheque or postal order payable to the ‘Driving Standards Agency’. You can’t pay by cash.

Download ‘Postal application for a driving theory test’ form (PDF, 356KB)

Data protection

You can read the Data Protection Act 1998 - Fair Processing Notice for the DSA before you start your booking.
