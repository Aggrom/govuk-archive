Guide to the approved driving instructor register

The guide to the approved driving instructor (ADI) register (known as the 'ADI14') sets out what's involved in becoming an ADI.

Only applies to England, Scotland and Wales

Introduction

When you’re applying to become an ADI you have to sign to say that you’ve read this document.

It tells you everything you need to know about:

  • the qualification and registration process
  • the role and powers of the ADI Registrar
  • the role of the Driving Standards Agency (DSA)
