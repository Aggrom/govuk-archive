Service Book your instructor theory test

Book your approved driving instructor (ADI) part 1 test, large goods vehicle (LGV) instructor theory test or register of post-test motorcycle trainers (RPMT) theory test.

What you need to know

Start now on the Directgov website

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know
  • Other ways to apply

You’ll need:

  • your driving licence number
  • your personal reference number from the Driving Standards Agency (DSA)
  • an email address for your booking confirmation to be sent to - if you don’t have an email address you can’t book online

It costs:

  • £90 for an ADI part 1 test
  • £66 for an LGV instructor theory test
  • £66 for an RPMT theory test

There’s a different process to book your ADI part 1 test in Northern Ireland.

  • Takes around 10 minutes
  • Debit or credit card required

Book by phone

You can book your instructor theory test by calling the DSA helpline.

DSA theory test helpline

Telephone: 0300 200 1122 (English), 0300 200 1133 (Welsh)
Textphone: 0300 200 1166
Monday to Friday, 8am to 4pm

Data protection

You can read the Data Protection Act 1998 - Fair Processing Notice for the DSA before you start your booking.
