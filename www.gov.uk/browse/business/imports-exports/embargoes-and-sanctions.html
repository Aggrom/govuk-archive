Embargoes and sanctions

Trade restrictions on exports of arms, military goods and Dual-Use Items to individual countries.

  1. Arms embargo on Armenia and Azerbaijan

    To export any controlled goods from the UK to Armenia and Azerbaijan, you need an export licence from the Export Control Organisation.

  2. Arms embargo on Burma

    Sanctions, arms embargo and other restrictions on exporting goods to Burma.

  3. Arms embargo on Democratic People's Republic of Korea (North Korea)

    There is an arms embargo on the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (North Korea), and an embargo on Dual-Use items which could be used for weapons of mass destruction.

  4. Arms embargo on Eritrea

    Export licences to Eritrea will not be issued unless they fit one of the exemptions set out in the embargo, such as export for humanitarian use or for use of UN personnel.

  5. Arms embargo on Sierra Leone

    How to apply for an export licence for arms, ammunition, weapons and related goods going to Sierra Leone.

  6. Arms embargo on the Democratic Republic of Congo

    The ban on the export of arms and related material to the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) means export controls are in force
