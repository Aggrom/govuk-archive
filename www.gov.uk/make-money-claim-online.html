Service Make a money claim online

You can take court action against someone if they owe you money and won’t pay you back.

Apply using your Government Gateway ID. If you don’t have one, register first using this service.

What you need to know

Start now on the Money Claim Online website

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know
  • Other ways to apply

How to register

Select ‘register as an individual’ or ‘register as an organisation’ on the Money Claim Online home page. To register you will need to enter your full name and provide a password.

What you can claim for

You can use this service to claim:

  • for a fixed sum under £100,000
  • against no more than 2 people or organisations

You will have to pay a court fee, using a debit or credit card.

What you can’t claim for

You can’t use this service to get:

  • compensation for an accident or injury
  • your tenancy deposit back from your landlord
  • Takes around 30 minutes
  • You must be over 18
  • Available in England and Wales only
  • Debit or credit card required

You can also apply by post.

You’ll need to fill in a claim form and return it to the County Court Money Claims Centre.

If you need help or advice, call or email the Money Claim Online help desk.
