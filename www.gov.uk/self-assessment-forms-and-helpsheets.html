Quick answer Self Assessment forms and helpsheets

Download forms and helpsheets for the past 4 tax years.

The deadline for paper tax returns is 31 October. You have until 31 January if you send your return online.

2011 to 2012 tax year

Download the forms and helpsheets appropriate for your situation:

  • Individuals (including the self-employed) tax return (form SA100)

  • Partnership tax return (form SA800)

  • Trust and estate tax return (form SA900)

  • Non-resident companies tax return (form SA700)

  • Trustees of registered pension schemes tax return (form SA970)
