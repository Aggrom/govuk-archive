Licence Animal movement licence (Scotland)

It is not currently possible to apply online for this licence. Please contact your local authority.

Overview

If you move animals on to or from a holding you must do so under the terms of the general movement licence issued by the Scottish Government Rural Payments and Inspections Directorate. If the conditions of this licence are not met then the right to move livestock may be suspended and an individual movement licence will be required.
