Quick answer Register to book driving tests as a trainer or business

Register with the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) so you can book certain types of theory and practical driving tests for your pupils more easily.

There are 2 ways you can register with the DSA:

  • as a business
  • as a trainer booker

Register as a business

If you register as a business and give your payment details, it means that you don’t have to give them each time you make a practical test booking.

You can also manage your availability for driving tests if you’re an approved driving instructor.

To register, download the business registration form and send it to the DSA.

Register as a trainer booker

When you register as a trainer booker:

  • you can book and pay for test slots in advance without giving your pupil’s details until nearer the test date
  • you don’t have to give your payment details each time you book test slots

Tests you can book as a trainer booker

As a trainer booker you can book practical tests for:

  • motorcycles
  • lorries and buses
  • Driver Certificate of Professional Competence (CPC)
  • car and trailers

You can also book theory tests for:

  • cars
  • motorcycles
  • lorries and buses
  • Driver CPC
  • approved driving instructor part 1

How to register as a trainer booker

To register you need to:

  1. Download the business registration form and send it to the DSA - this will register you for practical tests.

  2. Download the theory test application form and send it to the DSA - this will register you for theory tests.
