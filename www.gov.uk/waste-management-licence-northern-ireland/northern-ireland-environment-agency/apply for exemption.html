How to apply for exemption

There is more than one online form available for this licence:

  1. Application to register for waste management simple exemptions - WMXS
  2. Application to register an exempt activity: Paragraph 9, Waste Treatment on Land where such Treatment results in Benefit to Agriculture or Ecological Improvement.
  3. Application to register an exempt activity: Paragraph 10, The Spreading, or Storage and Spreading of sludge on land which is not land used for agriculture, within the meaning of the1990 Regulations.
  4. Application to register an exempt activity: Paragraph 10, The Storage of Sludge to be Spread under The Sludge (use in Agriculture) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 1990.
  5. Application to register an exempt activity: Paragraph 11, Spreading Waste for the Purpose of Reclamation, Restoration or Improvement of Land which has been subject to industrial/man made development
  6. Application to register for a waste exemption for composting and storage of biodegradable waste - paragraph 13
  7. Application to register an exempt activity: Paragraph 14, The Manufacture of Products from Waste, the Treatment of Waste Soil or Rock which is to be Spread on Land under Paragraph 9 or 11
  8. Application to register an exempt activity: Paragraph 15, The Manufacture of Finished Goods from Waste Metal, Plastic, Glass, Ceramics, Rubber, Textiles, Wood, Paper or Cardboard
  9. Application to register an exempt activity: Paragraph 19, Use and/or Storage of Waste for the purpose of Construction, Maintenance or Improvement of Relevant Work.
  10. Application to register an exempt activity: Paragraph 45, Recovery of Scrap Metal or Dismantling of Depolluted End-of-Life Vehicles.
  11. Application to register an exempt activity: Paragraph 49, The Repair, Refurbishment, and Storage of Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment (WEEE).
  12. Application to register an exempt activity: Paragraph 50, The Secure Storage of Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment (WEEE).
  13. Application to register an exempt activity: Schedule 2, Part I, Paragraph 51, The Crushing of Waste Gas Discharge Lamps.
  14. Application to register an exempt activity: Schedule 2, Part I, Paragraph 52, The Secure Storage of Waste Batteries or Accumulators
