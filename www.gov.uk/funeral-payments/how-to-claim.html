Part 4: How to claim

You must claim a Funeral Payment from the date of death and up to 3 months after the date of the funeral. If you’re waiting for a decision on getting a benefit you must still claim within the time limits.

You can make a claim before the funeral, if the funeral director can provide an invoice - an estimate is not acceptable.

Download form SF200, ‘Funeral Payment’ (PDF, 122KB)

The form comes with notes to help you fill it in and tell you where to send it.

Apply by phone

If you’re the surviving husband, wife or civil partner you can make a claim over the phone by contacting the Bereavement Service. They’ll help you claim any funeral costs or bereavement benefits you may be entitled to.
