Part 4: How to claim

You can apply for Pension Credit:

  • by post - send it to your local pension centre
  • by phone

You can download form PC1F, fill in the boxes and print it out, or you can download form PC1, print it out and write in the boxes.

What you need to claim

You will need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • information about your income, savings and investments
  • your bank account details

The earliest you can start your application is 4 months before you reach Pension Credit qualifying age.

You can claim any time after you reach Pension Credit qualifying age but your claim can only be backdated for 3 months.

If your circumstances change

Phone the helpline if your circumstances change as this can affect how much you get. For example, if you income or capital goes up or down.
