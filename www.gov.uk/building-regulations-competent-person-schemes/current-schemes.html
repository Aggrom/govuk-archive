Types of building work

Air pressure testing of buildings

The authorised scheme is BINDT. It has 2 parts and you can join either - one is for dwellings only and one is for any type of building.

To test any type of building you need to be a member of the Air Tightness testing and Measurement Association.

Cavity wall insulation in an existing building

The authorised scheme is CIGA.

Combustion appliances

Type of appliance Scheme(s)
Gas GSR
Oil (including liquid biofuels) APHC (not for appliances over 100 kilowatts output or in buildings more than 3 storeys high), BESCA, ELECSA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC
Solid fuel (including biomass) APHC (not for appliances over 100 kilowatts output or in buildings more than 3 storeys high), BESCA, ELECSA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC

Electrical installations

Type of installation Schemes
In dwellings - any electrical installation work, including lighting systems Benchmark, BESCA, BSI, ELECSA, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
In dwellings - ‘defined competence schemes’ - if you do electrical installation work in connection with your main work activity (eg bathroom or kitchen installation) APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, ELECSA, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
Buildings other than dwellings - lighting systems BESCA, ELECSA, NAPIT, NICEIC, Stroma

Heating and hot water systems

Type of system Schemes
Connected to a heat-producing gas appliance APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, ELECSA, GSR, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
Connected to an oil-fired combustion appliance APHC, BESCA, ELECSA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
Connected to a solid fuel appliance APHC, BESCA, ELECSA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
Connected to an electric heat source APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, ELECSA, HETAS, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
Electric heating systems - dwellings Benchmark, BESCA, BSI, ELECSA, NAPIT, NICEIC, OFTEC, Stroma
Electric heating systems - buildings other than dwellings BESCA, ELECSA, NAPIT, NICEIC, Stroma

Mechanical ventilation and air-conditioning systems

Type of building Schemes
Dwellings (not including work on systems shared with other buildings BESCA, NAPIT, NICEIC
Other buildings (not involving work on other parts of a building occupied separately) BESCA, NICEIC

Plumbing and water supply systems

Type of system Schemes
Wholesome and softened wholesome water supply APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, NAPIT, NICEIC
Non-wholesome water supply to a flushing toilet (not involving work on shared or underground drainage) APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, NAPIT, NICEIC
Toilets, sinks, washbasins, fixed baths, showers or bathrooms in dwellings (not involving work on shared or underground drainage) APHC, Benchmark, BESCA, NAPIT, NICEIC

Replacement windows, doors, roof windows or rooflights

Type of building Schemes
Existing dwellings BM TRADA, BSI, Certass, Fensa, Network VEKA
Existing buildings other than dwellings (not including replacement of load-bearing structural glass, glazed curtain walling or revolving doors) BM TRADA, Fensa

Replacement of roof coverings on pitched and flat roofs

This includes any necessary connected work (but not solar panel installation). The authorised scheme is NFRC.

NFRC operates the scheme under the name ‘CompetentRoofer’. A trade member of NFRC isn’t necessarily a member of CompetentRoofer.
