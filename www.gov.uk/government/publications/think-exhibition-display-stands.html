Detail

These displays are free to hire, but fulfilment of any order depends on availability. Please order at least 10 working days in advance of your event. Please see each individual download for images of the THINK! displays.

Ordering Details:

  • exhibition display dimensions: 2 metres x 2.8 metres (covers an approx. area of 2.3 metres x 3 metres)
  • the maximum order quantity for displays is 2
  • the maximum period that you can borrow the displays for is 3 weeks
  • during Christmas and busy periods, display boards need to be restricted to 1 week and 1 per local authority.
  • only 1 of each type of display can be ordered at a time. (e.g 1 drink drive, 1 mobile phone)
  • the displays are not robust enough to be used outside and we will not accept orders where they are being used in outside locations
  • the displays must be returned in the same condition as when you received them. The cost of any repairs will be charged to the person borrowing the display

To order a display, please email dftpublicity@dft.gsi.gov.uk. Please put “exhibition display order” in the subject heading. In your email, you must supply all of the following details: Whether you will erect and dismantle the kit(s) or you would like a contractor to do this

  • name
  • delivery address
  • telephone number
  • email address
  • second contact name and number
  • name of exhibition display required (ie drink drive, mobiles etc.)
  • reference number of the display (see table below)
  • where the kit(s) will be displayed, eg library, council offices, marquee, shopping centre (no outside venues please)
  • delivery date
  • collection date
  • whether you will erect and dismantle the kit(s) or you would like a contractor to do this
