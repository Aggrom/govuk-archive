Detail

This release provides accurate information on parishes and charter trustees in England for the financial year 2012-13.

  • In England in 2012-13 there are 9,967 parishes, 17 charter trustees and 2 Temples of London. This gives a total of 9,986 local precepting authorities.
  • Of the 9,984 parishes and charter trustees in England, 9,065 are charging a non-zero precept in 2012-13, 50 fewer than the previous year, or a decrease of 0.5 per cent.
  • The average Band D parish precept in England in 2012-13 is £47.74. This is an increase of £1.76, or 3.8 per cent over 2011-12.

Revisions to 2010-11 and 2011-12 data arising from a number of authorities correcting previously reported data are also included in the release and in the tables below.
