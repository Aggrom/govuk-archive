Detail

This report presents the department’s updated forecasts of the UK air passengers and UK aviation CO2 emissions to 2050. It is part of the response to the Committee on Climate Change (CCC) report on options for reducing CO2 emissions from UK aviation to 2050.

The conclusions of an independent peer review of the methods underpinning the updated forecasts, and associated technical notes, appear as supporting documents to the technical report.
