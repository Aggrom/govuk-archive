Detail

The tables here relate to the supply and performance on housing related support services in England, for Quarter 2 (Financial Year) 2010.

The information is collected on a quarterly basis, from 152 top-tier Administering Authorities, via the Supporting People Local System (SPLS) data uploads. The quarterly analysis provides a breakdown of:

  • the number of individual housing related support services, that each authority administers
  • the capacity of those services, measured in households units
  • the percentage of vulnerable people achieving independent living and supported to maintain independent living

Each of the supply analyses represents a snapshot of the number and capacity of services available at a particular point in time and does not reflect the number of households who have actually accessed those services. In addition, the quarterly performance analyses relate to an individual quarter and do not provide a cumulative picture.
