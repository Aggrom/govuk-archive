Summary

In October 2008, Arup and Oxera were appointed by the Department for Transport to undertake the study, ‘Rail Passenger Demand Forecasting: Revisiting the Elasticity-Based Framework’. The objective of this study is to re-estimate the background growth (economic, socio-demographic and land-use, and intermodal competition) and fare elasticities used in passenger demand forecasting.
