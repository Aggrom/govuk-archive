Detail

Summary

The department is conducting a trial of up to 1800 semi-trailers: up to 900 of a maximum length increase of 1m (between 13.6 and 14.6 metres), and up to 900 of a maximum length increase of 2.05m (between 14.6 and 15.65 metres). This Guidance informs potential applicants of how they can take part in the trial.

Consultation

This trial is a direct result of: Consultation on the possibility of allowing an increase in the length of articulated lorries
