Circular, letter or bulletin Roads circulars

Organisations:
  • Department
    for Transport
Published:
24 August 2005
Policies:
Managing, improving and investing in the road network and Improving local transport
Series:
Roads circulars

The 'Roads' circulars provided roads and traffic advice to local authorities.

Detail

For many years the Department for Transport’s predecessors used the circulars series ‘Roads’ to provide advice and guidance on roads and traffic matters to local authorities. The department no longer issues new circulars in this series. Advice on roads and traffic matters is now issued in various forms according to the nature of the advice and the audience, for example:

  • Former DTLR circulars (available from The Stationery Office )
  • Traffic advisory leaflets
  • Local transport notes (available from The Stationery Office)
  • Highways Agency

List of current circulars ‘Roads’

This is a list of all individual circulars from the ‘Roads’ series that are still current, in whole or in part. Any circulars in the ‘Roads’ series not listed are hereby cancelled.

In future, when one of these circulars is cancelled, it will be removed from the website. We will update this publication on the website to give the date of the cancellation and a link to any advice that replaces the cancelled circular.
