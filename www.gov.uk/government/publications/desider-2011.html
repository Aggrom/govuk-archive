Detail

desider is the monthly corporate magazine for Defence Equipment and Support. It is aimed at readers across DE&S, the wider MoD and Armed Forces and industry and covers stories and features about support to operations, equipment acquisition and support. It also covers the work of people in DE&S and its partners in industry and other corporate news and information.
