Parliamentary Under Secretary of State for Intellectual Property

  • The Rt Hon Lord Marland

Responsibilities

The minister is responsible for the business of the Department for Business, Innovation and Skills in the House of Lords. They are also responsible for intellectual property and the Intellectual Property Office.

Current role holder: The Rt Hon Lord Marland

Lord Marland was appointed Parliamentary Under Secretary of State for Intellectual Property in September 2012. He is a Conservative member of the House of Lords.

Education

He was educated at Shrewsbury School.

Political career

Lord Marland was Opposition Whip and Opposition Spokesperson for the Cabinet Office from 2009 to 2010. He was Parliamentary Under Secretary of State for the Department of Energy & Climate Change from May 2010 to September 2012.

Career outside of politics

Lord Marland was one of the founding directors of Jardine Lloyd Thomson plc, a multinational insurance business. He also founded the Jubilee insurance company at Lloyd’s of London. He is Chairman of UK Trade and Investment’s Business Ambassadors’ Group.

Personal life

Lord Marland is married to Penny, a probation officer. They have 4 children.
