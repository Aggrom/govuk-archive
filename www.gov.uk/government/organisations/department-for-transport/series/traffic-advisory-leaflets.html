Obtaining copies of traffic advisory leaflets

TALs published after 1 January 2011 are only available in electronic format. Printed copies will not be produced and are not available from DfT Publications.

Subject to availability, printed copies of TALs published before 1 January 2011 are available free of charge from DfT Publications. TALs will not be reprinted once existing stocks have been exhausted. When orsering printed copies, please state the quantity, title and reference number of each publication that you require, eg 2 copies of HIGH OCCUPANCY VEHICLE LANES (TAL 03/06).
