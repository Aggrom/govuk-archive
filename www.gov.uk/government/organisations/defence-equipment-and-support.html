The MOD’s Defence Equipment and Support department (DE&S) has a £14 billion budget to buy all the equipment and services that the army, navy and airforce need to operate effectively. We work closely with industry through partnering agreements and private finance initiatives.

Responsibilities

We are responsible for:

  • the procurement of ships, aircraft, vehicles, weapons, information systems and satellite communications
  • general requirements, food, clothing, medical and temporary accommodation
  • HM naval bases
  • the joint support chain
  • British Forces Post Office
  • Submarine Dismantling Project (SDP)

Priorities

In 2012 and 2013 our priorities include:

  • providing continual support for project teams managing current and future operations
  • acting as an objective decision-maker for the right equipment and services
  • recruiting over 20 technician apprentices in 2012
  • dismantling 27 nuclear submarines by 2050

Who we are

Defence Equipment & Support employs approximately 17,400 people around the UK and overseas and our headquarters are in Bristol.
