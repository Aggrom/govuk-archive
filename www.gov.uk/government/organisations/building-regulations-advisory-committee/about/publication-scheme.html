Publication scheme

Our publication scheme specifies the information that we publish and explains how to get that information.

We publish:

  • annual reports
  • guidance to BRAC members (in the BRAC members’ handbook)
  • agendas, papers and minutes of BRAC and its working parties
  • a list of BRAC members

Published information is available for at least a year from its date of publication.

Exempt information

The publication scheme doesn’t include information that is protected under the exemptions in Part II of the Freedom of Information Act 2000.

Requesting information

Information included under the publication scheme will generally be available on this website.

To get copies of BRAC agendas, papers, and minutes, and any other correspondence between DCLG officials and BRAC members, contact the BRAC Secretariat.

When requesting information from the BRAC Secretariat please include:

  • your name and address (and organisation, if applicable)
  • details of the information or documents you would like to see
  • the format you would like the information to be in (we will attempt to provide the information in the format you would like, but this may not be possible in all cases)

Charges

BRAC may charge for the retrieval, photocopying and postage of material. We will let you know of any charge (payable in advance) when you make your request.
