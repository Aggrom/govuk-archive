What we do

Department of Energy & Climate Change (DECC) is a ministerial department

Read more about DECC

See all of our agencies & partners

We focus on these topics

  1. Energy

    Oil and gas reserves are declining but the UK must have safe, secure and future-proof supplies of energy that are available to all. Through international relations and supporting the energy industry, we will ensure a continuous supply of energy for the UK.

  2. Environment and climate change

    The world faces significant challenges from the effects of pollution. By helping other countries tackle excessive CO2 emissions and making the UK’s economy greener we will protect our biodiversity and safeguard the future of our finite natural resources.
