Closed consultation Public consultation on Senior Traffic Commissioner's statutory guidance and statutory directions

Organisations:
  • HM Government
  • Department
    for Transport
  • Traffic Commissioners
Published:
9 November 2011
Policy:
Managing, improving and investing in the road network

This consultation ran between 9 November 2011 and 22 November 2011

We are analysing your responses

Visit this page again soon to download the outcome to this public feedback.

Detail

The Local Transport Act 2008 requires the Senior Traffic Commissioner to provide guidance and directions as to the way in which the traffic commissioners will approach the exercise of their statutory functions. The existing guidance notes and practice directions have been reviewed and revised, which is the subject of this consultation.
