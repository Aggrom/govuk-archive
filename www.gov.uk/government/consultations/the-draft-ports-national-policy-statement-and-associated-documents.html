Detail

Summary

The Department for Transport published this consultation document on 9 November 2009 in order to invite views on the draft National Policy Statement (NPS) for ports and the accompanying documents listed below.

This consultation document explained the context of the NPS and set out the key policies. The consultation period for the draft NPS closed on 15 February 2010. The responses are now being carefully considered and the department will publish a summary of them.

The NPS has been under parliamentary scrutiny and the Transport Select Committee published a report to the House in March (HC 217).

The Secretary of State will lay a response before Parliament in due course, as required by s.9(5) Planning Act 2008.
