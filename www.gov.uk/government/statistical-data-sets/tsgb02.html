Statistical data set TSGB02

Organisations:
  • Department
    for Transport
Published:
16 November 2012
Series:
Transport Statistics Great Britain

This chapter of Transport Statistics Great Britain presents statistics on activity at UK airports, including number of flights, passengers and volume of freight handled; flight punctuality; passenger characteristics and origins/destinations for passengers on international flights.
