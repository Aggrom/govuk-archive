Statistical data set Live tables on house price index

Organisations:
  • Department for
    Communities and
    Local Government
Published:
10 November 2012
Series:
Housing market

The live tables provide the latest, most useful or most popular data, presented by type and other variables, including by geographical area or as a time series.

These live tables show the latest mix-adjusted house prices, house price annual rate of change figures and simple average house prices, mortgages and incomes. More details, including methodology and details about the monthly house price index, are in Housing market and house price information: notes and definitions.
