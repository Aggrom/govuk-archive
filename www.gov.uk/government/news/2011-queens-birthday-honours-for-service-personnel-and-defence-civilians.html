News 2011: Queen's Birthday Honours for Service personnel and Defence civilians

Organisations:
Ministry
of Defence
Published:
13 June 2011

126 UK Service personnel have been granted state honours by Her Majesty The Queen in her annual Birthday Honours list.

43 civilians have also been honoured either for work in the Ministry of Defence or in other aspects of UK Defence.

The full Defence Birthday Honours list for 2011 is below:
