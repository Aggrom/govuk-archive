News Queen's 2012 Birthday Honours for Service personnel and Defence civilians

Organisations:
  • Ministry
    of Defence
Published:
16 June 2012

109 UK Service personnel have been granted state honours by Her Majesty The Queen in her annual Birthday Honours List.

40 civilians have also been honoured either for work in the Ministry of Defence or in other aspects of UK Defence.

The full Defence Birthday Honours List for 2012 is below:
