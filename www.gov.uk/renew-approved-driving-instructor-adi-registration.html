Service Renew your approved driving instructor (ADI) registration

Not what you're looking for? ↓

Renew your approved driving instructor (ADI) registration if it’s about to run out, or re-register if it ran out in the last 12 months.

What you need to know

Start now on the Department for Transport website

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know
  • Other ways to apply

It costs £300.

The online service is available from 6am to 11:30pm.

Photocard driving licence

If you have a photocard driving licence you’ll need:

  • your online user ID from the Driving Standards Agency (DSA)
  • your password
  • your driving licence number (if you’re giving DSA permission to use your photocard photo)
  • your criminal record check disclosure

Paper driving licence

If you have an old-style paper driving licence you’ll also need:

  • to print the final screen and send it to DSA
  • a passport-sized photo with your ADI personal reference number written on the back

Your user ID and password

Your user ID can be found on all letters sent to you by DSA. It’s not the same as your ADI personal reference number.

You can email DSA if you’ve never had a user ID or have lost yours. You’ll need to include 2 of these in your email:

  • your ADI number
  • your driving licence number
  • your date of birth

DSA helpline

irdt@dsa.gsi.gov.uk

Rules for passwords

You have to change your password every 60 days. You can’t use any of your 5 previously used passwords.

Your password must be at least 9 characters long. It must include at least 3 of these 4 types of characters:

  • uppercase characters, eg A B C
  • lowercase characters, eg a b c
  • numeric digits, eg 1 2 3
  • special characters, eg £ % &

There’s a different process to renew your ADI registration in Northern Ireland.

  • Takes around 15 minutes
  • Not available in Northern Ireland
  • Debit or credit card required
  • Digital or signed photo required

Apply by post

To renew your ADI registration or re-register by post, download the correct application form and send it to DSA.

You’ll need to include a passport-sized photo with your ADI personal reference number written on the back, and the correct fee.

Renew your registration

Use form ‘ADI 37a’ if your registration is about to run out.

Download ‘ADI 37a - Application for extension of registration as an ADI’ (PDF, 352KB)

Re-register

Use form ‘ADI 37b’ if your registration ran out in the last 12 months.

Download ‘ADI 37b - Application for re-registration as an ADI’ (PDF, 412KB)
