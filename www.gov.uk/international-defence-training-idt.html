Introduction

The UK has a long and proud tradition of delivering Defence training of the highest quality to its own Armed Forces. Training is available to other nations via the four Intenational Defence Training organisations.

It is also a matter of UK government policy to offer training to our allies and other countries which share our goal of securing a more peaceful world. In most cases international personnel will have the opportunity to train alongside British service personnel and civilians.

Our training establishments run many hundreds of courses and by clicking on the links on this page you will be able to access online course details for all three services. Every course is described along with details of how to apply, the required fitness and English language levels and points of contact.

If you would like further information about how we can meet your training needs our individual Service International Defence Training staff will be delighted to help.

International Defence Training (Army) (IDT(A))

The British Army has a long and proud tradition of offering training opportunities to international military students, supporting UK security cooperation objectives. The British Army’s force development and training organisation is highly professional and recognised as a world leader in many areas of military skills and capability.

IDT(A) Mission

IDT(A) is part of the Army Recruiting and Flag Officer Sea Training (FOST)Training Division (ARTD) and is responsible for:

  • coordinating delivery of IDT activity on individual courses and through other training activity to international military students at Army Training establishments and Defence Colleges within ARTD
  • administering International military students attending courses at Royal Military Academy Sandhurst, Joint Services Command and Staff College and Defence Academy - College of Management and Technology
  • providing training advice and information to UK and Foreign Defence and Military Attaches in order to contribute to MOD delivery of international security cooperation objectives.

International Defence Training (Royal Navy) (IDT(RN))

International Defence Training (Royal Navy) provides training of the highest quality to the international defence community and maritime organisations.

Flag Officer Sea Training (FOST) as the Chief of Staff (Training) has responsibilty for all Royal Navy (RN) and Royal Fleet Auxiliary (RFA) training.

This website and the associated catalogue provide readily accessible information on training available to the international community including:

  • new entry training
  • career courses in warfare and engineering
  • Naval aviation
  • Royal Marines
  • flexible training options including mobile sea training
  • operational sea training

IDT(RN) is the focal point for international requests and all enquiries are welcome.

Related information

The IDT(RN) Catalogue shows a variety of Royal Navy courses available to the international defence community and maritime organisations; for further details please contact our team.

It is possible on occasion to arrange bespoke courses in the UK and overseas; please contact our team to discuss your training needs. Contact us
