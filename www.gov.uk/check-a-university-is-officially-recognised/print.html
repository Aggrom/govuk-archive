Check if a university or college is officially recognised, a guide from GOV.UK

Notes

Part 1: Overview

Higher learning institutions that are officially recognised in the UK are called either ‘recognised’ or ‘listed’ bodies.

Recognised bodies are institutions that can award their own degrees.

Listed bodies offer courses that can lead to a degree from a recognised body.

If your degree’s awarded in the UK, it must come from a recognised or listed body - if it doesn’t, it will be considered bogus and won’t count when you’re looking for a job.
