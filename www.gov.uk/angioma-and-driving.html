Quick answer Angioma and driving

You must tell DVLA if you have angiomas or cavernomas.

You can be fined up to £1,000 if you don’t tell DVLA about a medical condition that affects your driving. If you’re involved in an accident, you may be prosecuted.

Car, motorcycle, bus, coach or lorry licence

You must tell DVLA. Fill in form B1 and send it to DVLA. The address is on the form.
