Part 1: Overview

If you’ve been claiming benefit you could get Return to Work Credit if:

  • you’ve been unable to work for at least 13 weeks because of an illness or disability
  • you’re going back to work, starting a new job or self-employment

You have to qualify for Return to Work Credit. Any money you get won’t affect your other benefits and is tax-free.

To get Return to Work Credit you must:

  • tell Jobcentre Plus you’ve started work within 5 weeks of doing so
  • have started claiming benefit before 2 July 2013
  • start work or self-employment before 1 October 2013.
