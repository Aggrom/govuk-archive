Part 6: Help and advice with disciplinary issues

There are several organisations that could give you advice about disciplinary issues.

Acas

Acas (the Advisory, Conciliation and Arbitration Service) offers free, confidential and impartial advice about all employment rights issues.

Acas
Telephone: 08457 474747
Text relay: 18001 08457 474747
8am to 8pm Monday to Friday, Saturday 9am to 1pm

Citizens Advice Bureau (CAB)

Your local Citizens Advice Bureau can also give free and impartial advice.

Trade unions

If you’re a member of a trade union you can get help and advice from them.

Pay and Work Rights Helpline

The Pay and Work Rights Helpline has free advice about your employment rights.

Pay and Work Rights Helpline
Telephone: 0800 917 2368
Textphone: 0800 121 4042
9am to 5pm, Monday to Friday

Equality and Human Rights Commission

Contact the Equality and Human Rights Commission for advice about discrimination, equality and human rights.

Equality and Human Rights Commission
3 More London Riverside
Tooley Street
London
SE1 2RG
Telephone: 0845 604 6610 (England), 0845 604 5510 (Scotland), 0845 604 8810 (Wales)
Textphone: 0845 604 6620 (England), 0845 604 5520 (Scotland), 0845 604 8820 (Wales)
8am to 6pm, Monday to Friday

Other organisations

Community Legal Advice is a free phone line which can tell you if you could get legal aid to help with employment issues.

Community Legal Advice
Telephone: 0845 345 4 345
Textphone: 0845 609 6677
9am to 8pm Monday to Friday, 9am to 12.30pm Saturday
