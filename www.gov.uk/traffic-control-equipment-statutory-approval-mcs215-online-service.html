Traffic Control Equipment Statutory Approval (MCS215) online service

How to access and use the register of type approved and self-certified traffic control equipment, maintained by the Highways Agency.

Contents

  1. Introduction
  2. Further information
  3. See more like this

Introduction

The Highways Agency maintains a register of type approved and self-certified traffic signal display equipment. This register - document MCS215 - can be accessed online by registered users.

Register as a new user on the Traffic Control Equipment Statutory Approval (MCS215) website.

After you have set up your password, you can log in to the service.

Log in to the Traffic Control Equipment Statutory Approval (MCS215) online service (registration required).

How to search the register

You can search MCS215 by various criteria:

  • approval type
  • equipment group
  • supplier name
  • equipment number
  • specification number
  • specification appendix letter
  • approval number
  • approval date
  • MRI issue number
  • issue date
  • approval status

The search results show the above information for each item of equipment.
