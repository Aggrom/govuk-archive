Service Book, change and cancel theory tests for your trainee instructors

Not what you're looking for? ↓

Book and manage the approved driving instructor part 1 test for your trainee instructors. You’ll need to be registered to use the trainer booking facility for theory tests.

What you need to know

Start now on the theory test booking service

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know

You’ll also need the following from the Driving Standards Agency (DSA):

  • business ID
  • username
  • password

You can book test slots up to 3 months in advance. You won’t have to give the candidate’s details when you book the tests.

You must confirm the details of the candidate taking the test by 4pm a full working day before the test, eg by 4pm on Wednesday if the test is on Friday.

Sundays and public holidays are not counted as working days.

Cancel or change a test

You must give at least 3 clear working days notice when cancelling or changing a test to get a refund.

Data protection

You can read the Data Protection Act 1998 fair processing notice for DSA before you start.

  • Takes around 10 minutes
  • 3 years of address details required
  • Not available in Northern Ireland
  • Debit or credit card required
