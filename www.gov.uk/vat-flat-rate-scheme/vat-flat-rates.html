Part 4: VAT flat rates

The rate is a percentage of your business turnover. The actual rate depends on your business type. If the rate changes, you must apply the new rate from the date it changes.

In your first year as a VAT-registered business the rate is reduced by 1% until the day before your registration anniversary.
